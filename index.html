Rutvij's LA Fitness Plan

3-day split • 45 min lifting + 15–20 min treadmill • Keep form clean, last 2 reps hard

Day 1 – Chest + Triceps

Target: Chest (push) + Triceps • Total time ≈ 60 min
Warmup (5 min)

3 min easy treadmill walk + 2 sets of push-ups on wall or bench (10 reps).

Finisher – Treadmill (15–20 min)

Incline: 7% Speed: 2.8–3.2 mph Walk at a pace where you can still talk, but feel your heart working.
